What are the Solfeggio frequencies?

The Solfeggio frequencies are a set of tones (from 174 to 963 Hz) rooted in an ancient musical tradition, each historically linked to a different quality, from grounding and calm to focus and openness. They are not medicine: think of them as a soundscape to relax, focus or fall asleep to. Below you'll find what each one is traditionally associated with.

How to use them

Use headphones for the cleanest tone, keep the volume low and comfortable, and let the sound play in the background while you relax, work, study, do yoga or drift off to sleep. There is nothing to do: just listen and breathe. Sessions from 30 minutes to a few hours work well; pick the frequency whose theme fits your moment.
