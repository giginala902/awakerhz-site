What data we collect

We keep data collection to a strict minimum. The only personal data we collect directly is your email address, and only if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter using the subscription form on the site. We do not require accounts, logins, or registration to use the website, there are no payments processed on this site, and we do not run first-party analytics or first-party tracking cookies. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for advertising or marketing by third parties. As with most websites, certain technical data (such as your IP address and browser information) may be processed automatically by the third-party services that help deliver the site, as described in the third parties section below.

Purpose and legal basis

We process your email address for a single purpose: to send you the AwakerHz newsletter, which may include updates about new frequency-based music and wellbeing content, releases, and related news. The legal basis for this processing is your consent, which you give by submitting your email through the newsletter form. Providing your email is entirely optional; you can use the website without subscribing. You may withdraw your consent at any time, for example by using the unsubscribe option in our emails or by contacting us at contact@awakerhz.com. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.

Cookies and similar technologies

The AwakerHz website does not set first-party tracking cookies and does not run first-party analytics. We do not use cookies to profile you or to track your activity across other sites. Some third-party services we rely on may set their own cookies or use similar technologies when their content loads, in particular embedded YouTube videos and Google Fonts; we use the privacy-enhanced youtube-nocookie domain to reduce this where possible. These technologies are governed by the respective providers' own cookie and privacy policies. You can also control or block cookies through your browser settings.

Third parties and possible data transfers

To run the site and the newsletter, we rely on a small number of third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy: Formspree, which receives and processes the newsletter form so that your email address reaches us; Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com), used to load the fonts that display the site, which may involve your browser connecting to Google servers; and embedded YouTube videos delivered through the privacy-enhanced youtube-nocookie domain, which loads video content when you view a page containing it. Some of these providers may process data outside the European Union or the European Economic Area; where that happens, such transfers are intended to be covered by appropriate safeguards (for example, the European Commission Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision). We do not use first-party tracking cookies and we do not sell your data.

Data retention

We keep your email address only for as long as you remain subscribed to the newsletter, that is, until you withdraw your consent or ask us to remove it. When you unsubscribe or request erasure, we delete your email from our active mailing list without undue delay, unless we are required to keep certain information for a limited period to comply with a legal obligation. Technical data handled automatically by the third-party services above is retained according to each provider's own retention practices, as set out in their respective privacy policies.

Your rights under the GDPR

Under the GDPR and applicable Italian law you have the following rights regarding your personal data: the right of access (to know what data we hold and obtain a copy); the right to rectification (to correct inaccurate or incomplete data); the right to erasure (to have your data deleted); the right to restriction of processing; the right to object to processing; the right to data portability (to receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format); and the right to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@awakerhz.com and we will respond within the time limits set by law. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular the Italian data protection authority, the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali (www.garanteprivacy.it), or the authority in your country of residence.

Children

This website and its content are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 16. If you are under 16, please do not submit your email address through the newsletter form. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at contact@awakerhz.com and we will delete it promptly.
